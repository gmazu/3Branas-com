What currently defines the framework

The current priority is not to “explain everything” at once, but to close pieces with mechanism: collision geometry, observational scales, medium perturbations, experimental analogies, and documents that can be read without depending on a previous conversation.

The collision is not treated as an explosion without mechanism, but as a physical interface sweeping a geometry.
eCEL is used as a liberated regime of the medium, with propagation, coupling, and wave-like response.
Apparent inflation is read geometrically, not as matter moving faster than light.
The same architecture tries to connect cosmological structure, gravitational waves, and more microscopic branches of the model.
